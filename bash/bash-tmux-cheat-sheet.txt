BASH:

Ctrl-A     jump to beginning of line.
Ctrl-E     jump to end of line.
Ctrl-K     delete one line.
Ctrl-Y     paste in last deleted line.
Ctrl-R     Reverse search.

TMUX:

SHIFT-b %                   split window vertical.
SHIFT-b Left/Right-Arrow    change to left or right window.
SHIFT-b c                   new session.
SHIFT-b d                   kill session.

SPECIAL-COMMANDS:
ack, jq
